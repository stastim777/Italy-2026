Thursday · Day 6 · Italy
Lemon Path + Sorrento
Morning: bus to Sant'Agata → walk back through lemon groves · rest of day play by ear
🍋 Path of Lemons morning🌿 Giardini di Cataldo option☀ Go early before the heat
8:00hidden gem
SITA bus to Sant'Agata → walk back through lemon groves
▼

Take the SITA bus from Piazza Tasso up to Sant'Agata sui Due Golfi (~20–30 min, a couple of euros). From there walk downhill ~5 km through the real Sorrentine countryside — lemon groves, olive trees, vineyards, small villages, the scent of Sfusato lemons everywhere. ~2 hrs mostly downhill, arriving back in Sorrento mid-morning.

Sant'Agata means "above both gulfs" — from the top you see the Gulf of Naples and Gulf of Salerno simultaneously. Go early before the heat. This is the direction locals recommend: always uphill by bus, downhill on foot.
10:30hidden gem
Optional: Giardini di Cataldo — working lemon farm in Sorrento
▼

A working lemon farm right in Sorrento on Via Fuorimura — 10-min walk from the apartment. Walk through traditional pergola-covered lemon terraces (the characteristic Sorrentine cultivation style), learn how limoncello is made, and do a tasting. 30–45 min, flat pathways, family-friendly for any age. Small entry fee.

The Sfusato Sorrentino lemons here are enormous, thin-skinned, and almost sweet. The smell inside the pergola is extraordinary.

Italy and France are completely different for ride-sharing. Uber works like the US in France (full UberX). In Italy it's Uber Black only (expensive) — use local taxi apps instead. Download all four apps before leaving home.
Apps to download before departure
Free Now
Best cross-border app — Italy AND France. Licensed taxis, fare estimate upfront. Apple Pay accepted.
Italy + France · Download now
itTaxi
Italy's largest official taxi network. Nationwide including Bergamo. No surge pricing. English interface.
Italy only · Download now
Uber
Update and confirm Apple Pay. Full UberX in Aix + Marseille. Uber Black only in Milan + Rome. Not in Naples or Sorrento.
France main · Italy limited
Bolt
Cheaper than Uber in France. Good for Marseille/Aix area. Limited/unreliable in Italy.
France only · Optional
